    A project begins to take shape in the form of prototypes. We take the planned deliverable and determine which components are the most difficult to build and which represent the backbone of the solution.   • Collaboration

    Throughout the whole project we'd be working very closely with the customer to ensure that the deliverable will indeed meet the expectations and solve the problem. From experience we know certain aspects of a solution need to be flexible in design to work with changing requirements.

  • Delivery

    Depending on the complexity of the solution, this process could take as little as an hour to as long as six weeks. During this process we monitor the solution carefully to ensure that we catch any last minute issues and rectify them as quickly as possible. After that its time to start thinking about the next set of features and enhancements.
